FAQs:

What are Sunnies e-gift cards

The Sunnies e-gift card is a digital gift that comes in the form of special code.
It's the perfect gift for any of your friends and for any occasion.

How does it work?

Choose how much you want to give to your friend and proceed to checkout.
Once purchased, we'll send you and your friend an email with the gift code within 1-2 working days.

What can the Sunnies e-gift card purchase?

Anything from the Sunnies online! Old styles, new styles, flat lens, matte, everything that can be squeezed in the value of the card.

How much are the e-gift cards?

The Sunnies e-gift cards come in denominations of P100, P300, P500, and P1000 to fit any type of budgget. If your friend's checkout total is higher then value of the gift card you gave, your friend will need to shoulder the excess amount.

Can I give more than one e-gift card to the same friend?

You may!However, they may only use one e-gift card per transaction.

Can I purchase the Sunnies e-gift card from Sunnies stations?

Sunnies stations offer the Sunnies Gift Card which is different from the e-gift card. The gift card allows your friend to shop for their gift in-store only. With the e-gift card, they can shop online anywhere they are!

Whate if I want to send one to my entire phonebook

If you want to give away a ton of GCs to a group of 10 people or more, send us an email at help@sunniesstudios.com and we'll set something up for you.

Yey! A friend sent me an e-gift card! how do I use this?

Great! you have a friend who cares about your well-being. Pick a style online and proceed to check-out. Input the gift code in the promo box and wait for your pair to be delivered. Optional step: Thank your friend by sending them a selfie while sporting your new pair.

Will it expire?

Nope! They're valid forever!

Can I encash the e-gift Card

While they are good as money, the e-gift cards may not be exchanged for cash and are not refundable.

What happens to the rest of the e-gift card if I don't use it all in one go?

The special code may only be used once and once used any remaining amount will be lost in digital space.
